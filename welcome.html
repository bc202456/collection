THIS IS A WEBSITE ABOUT RECORDS

—my records, to be exact. Over the past decade, my grandfather and I have gone on annual record-hunting adventures to find music that speaks to us. This personal archive acts not only as a catalogue of an ever-growing collection, but as a testament to human connection and a bond that spans generations. I’ve selected four of my favorite albums, each meaningful in their own beautiful way. You might love them or hate them. Regardless, you will have understood something about me, my tastes, and the way my grandfather and I connected over an outdated medium of sound…or maybe you will just find a new song to like. To get started, click on a record icon on this page or “the records” on the navigation bar.
